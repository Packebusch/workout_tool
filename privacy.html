2. Overview

This Workout Tracker is a 100% client-side application. All your data is stored locally in your browser and never leaves your device.

3. Data Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Workout history (dates, reps, calories, workout types)
  • Streak information (consecutive workout days)
  • No personal identifying information is collected

4. No Server Communication

✓ No data is sent to any server
✓ No cookies are used
✓ No analytics or tracking
✓ No third-party services
✓ No personal information collected

5. Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View: All data is visible in the History panel
  • Export: Use browser developer tools to access localStorage
  • Delete: Use the "Clear All History" button to delete all data
  • Browser Clear: Clearing browser data will delete all workout history

6. Data Portability

To export your data:

  1. Open browser Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to Console tab
  3. Type: localStorage.getItem('burpeeWorkoutHistory')
  4. Copy the output to save your data

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally in your browser)
  • Delete your data (use "Clear All History" button)
  • Export your data (see Data Portability above)

8. Children's Privacy

This application does not collect any personal information and can be safely used by anyone. However, children should consult with a healthcare provider before starting any exercise program.
